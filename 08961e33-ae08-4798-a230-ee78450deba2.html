Welcome to the first theme of the course. There are three topics for this unit:

  • How do we study life in the Universe?
  • Cosmology: The History of the Universe
  • Stars: Energy for Life

The unit will be completed over two weeks of the course.  Except for some historical background, this is intended primarily to review material you covered in a previous introductory astronomy class, with some particular focus on ideas most relevant to the emergence of life in the Universe.  If this material is new to you, then please devote extra time to this theme.
