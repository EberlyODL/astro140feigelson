Throughout the semester, you will read the online "lectures" at this website and listen to ocassional videos.  

Starting in Week 3, we will be reading much of the required textbook:

An Introduction to Astrobiology, David A. Rothery et al. (editors), 2nd edition, 2011,Cambridge University Press, ISBN 978-1-107-60093-5 (paperback) http://www.cambridge.org/9781107600935

The book can be acquired from online booksellers including the publisher, Barnes & Noble, and Amazon.  This is an excellent resource where several British scientists join together to write highly informative chapters on most aspects of our course.  The text is more detailed than the online `lectures' and students do not need to memorize or study intensively all of its contents.  But it gives lots of substantive detail underlying the issues we discuss, and is a valuable complement to the lectures.   Please be sure to have the text in hand by Week 3.
