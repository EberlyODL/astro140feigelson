Here we will listen to a ~20 minute talk by Prof. Eric B. Ford from Penn State's Dept. of Astronomy & Astrophysics.  This is the video he presents at this point in the Astro 140 curriculum.   Eric is a prize-winning researcher in exoplanetary astronomy, and he has an thoughtful and deeply informed analysis of what we can ... and can not ... expect to discover about life on exoplanets.  I recommend that you first read our textbook's Chpt. 8 on 'How to find life on exoplanets', as it interweaves nicely with Eric's presentation.
